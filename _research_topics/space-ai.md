Space AI in our group focuses on **multi-temporal reasoning**, **physics-informed priors**, and **calibration under domain shift** when sensors, orbits, and illumination vary wildly. We study foundation-style encoders for large-scale tiles, interactive tools for analysts, and evaluation protocols that stress rare events such as wildfire fronts and flood expansion.

### In Cooperation With

Collaborations with remote-sensing groups on change detection benchmarks, uncertainty-aware fusion of SAR and optical streams, and lightweight models suitable for edge deployment on satellites and aerial platforms.
