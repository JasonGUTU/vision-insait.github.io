**Vision Agent** is our umbrella for **vision–language models**, **grounded tool use**, and **agentic workflows** that plan over images, video, and documents. We study calibration, long-context memory for episodic video, and interfaces that keep humans in the loop for high-stakes decisions.

### In Cooperation With

Cross-site projects on open-vocabulary segmentation, retrieval interfaces for curators, and red-teaming of vision-language systems with HCI partners.
