We study how machines infer stable 3D structure from images, video, and LiDAR—spanning **NeRF-style radiance fields**, **Gaussian splatting**, SLAM with loop closure, and correspondence learning for articulated objects. Applications include digital twins, mixed reality capture, and robotics-ready maps with semantic layers.

### In Cooperation With

We co-maintain public benchmarks on implicit surfaces and Gaussian splatting with partner labs, and host annual challenges on robust odometry under motion blur and rolling shutter.
