Egocentric vision asks models to **see with the user**: hands in frame, shifting gaze, and noisy microphones. We work on long-horizon anticipation, sound–vision fusion, and 3D scene graphs anchored to wearable cameras, with emphasis on fairness across environments and low-light robustness.

### In Cooperation With

Active data-sharing agreements with museums and assistive-living pilots; annotation partners for long-form narration, hand–object contact, and nighttime ego capture.
