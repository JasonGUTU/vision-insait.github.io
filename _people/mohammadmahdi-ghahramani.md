**About me:**

I am Mohammadmahdi and I am joining INSAIT as a PhD student in Computer Vision with advisers [Prof. Luc Van Gool](https://insait.ai/prof-luc-van-gool/) and [Dr. Danda Pani Paudel](https://insait.ai/dr-danda-paudel/). My research interests are in robotics & computer vision, generative AI, and reinforcement learning. During my Master’s program at the University of Padova, I conducted research under the supervision of [Prof. Lamberto Ballan](http://www.lambertoballan.net/). My work focused on developing RefDiff, a plug-in network designed for Stable Diffusion Models, which was specifically aimed at improving the visual grounding task using latent representations.
