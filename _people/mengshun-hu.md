**Postdoctoral researcher **

Email: [email protected]

I am a postdoc researcher at INSAIT, hosted by [Prof. Luc Van Gool](https://insait.ai/prof-luc-van-gool/) and [Dr. Jinjin Gu](https://insait.ai/dr-jinjin-gu/). Previously, I earned my Ph.D. in National Engineering Research Center for Multimedia Software at Wuhan University, China. Prior to this, I was also a visiting student at University of Tokyo, Japan, and completed a long-term internship at the Shanghai Al Laboratory. I also received my master’s degree in National Engineering Research Center for Multimedia Software at Wuhan University, China. Before that, I received my bachelor’s degree in Communication Engineering from JiangHan University, China.

My research interests lie in video restoration, particularly focusing on space-time video super-resolution, supported by Huawei MindSpore Academic Award Fund. Recently, I am working mainly on VideoAgent.
