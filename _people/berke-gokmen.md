**About Me:**

I’m a researcher at the Institute for Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence and Technology (INSAIT) working on 3D scene generation and generative AI under the supervision of Dr. Ajad Chhatkuli. My research explores text and image-conditioned 3D scene reconstruction/generation, diffusion models, and multimodal modeling for vision applications. I’m especially interested in controllable generation, layout reasoning, and scalable training pipelines for large vision models. I have previously published at NeurIPS and ICCV.

I recently graduated from [Bilkent University](https://w3.bilkent.edu.tr/bilkent/) with a B.Sc. in Computer Science (CGPA 3.91/4.0) as a Comprehensive Scholarship recipient. During my studies, I completed an exchange semester at EPFL and an internship at ETH Zürich, contributing to projects in generative computer vision. Before joining INSAIT, I spent two years as a software engineer, where I built production-grade systems and pipelines that now inform my research, from efficient data processing evaluation and deployment.
