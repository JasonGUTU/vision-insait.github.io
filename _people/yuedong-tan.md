I am a visiting Ph.D. studies at the Institute for Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence and Technology (INSAIT), under the supervision of Danda Pani Paudel and Luc Van Gool. I received my M.Eng. degree from Xidian University in June 2025 and my B.E. degree from Chang’an University, China, in July 2022. I have also worked as a research intern at TeleAI.
