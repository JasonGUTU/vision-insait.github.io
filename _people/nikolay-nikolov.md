**About me:**

I am Niko, a PhD student in Computer Vision and Robotics, working with [Prof. Luc Van Gool](https://insait.ai/prof-luc-van-gool/) and [Dr. Danda Pani Paudel](https://insait.ai/dr-danda-paudel/).
My work is at the intersection of Robotics, World Modeling and Reinforcement Learning. I am particularly interested in developing end-to-end foundational models for Robotics.

Previously, I was part of the core research team at Wayve, an autonomous driving company. There, I developed one of the first offline Reinforcement Learning models applied to autonomous driving in the real world. With my team we proved that end-to-end AI can drive in complex urban environments such as central London, which at the time was considered impossible by the entire industry.
