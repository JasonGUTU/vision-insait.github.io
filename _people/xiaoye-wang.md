**About Me:**

I am a Graduate Research Fellowship Student at the Institute for Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence and Technology (INSAIT), working on 3D computer vision under the supervision of [Prof. Luc Van Gool](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=TwMib_QAAAAJ&hl=en) and [Dr. Danda Paudel](https://scholar.google.ch/citations?user=W43pvPkAAAAJ&hl=en).

Previously, I was an MPhil student at the University of Cambridge, majoring in Data Intensive Science. During this time, I also worked remotely as a research intern at MMLab@CUHK.

Before that, I received my bachelor degree in 2024 from Harbin Institute of Technology. During my undergraduate studies, I was awarded the [Singapore International Pre-Graduate Award (SIPGA)](https://www.a-star.edu.sg/Scholarships/for-undergraduate-studies/singapore-international-pre-graduate-award-sipga) and worked as a research assistant at the Institute for Infocomm Research, A*STAR, and as a research intern at the School of Medicine, National University of Singapore.

My research interests span computer vision and machine learning, with a particular focus on 3D vision, generative models, and AI4Healthcare.
