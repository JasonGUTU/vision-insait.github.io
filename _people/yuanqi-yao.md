**About me:**

Hi, I’m Yuanqi Yao, a Ph.D. student at INSAIT – starting in Sep 2025, under the supervision of [Prof. Luc Van Gool](https://insait.ai/prof-luc-van-gool/) and [Dr. Danda Paudel](https://insait.ai/dr-danda-paudel/). My research focuses on Embodied AI, particularly robotic manipulation and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models. Welcome to discuss and collaborate!

Prior to this, I completed a long-term internship at the [Shanghai AI Laboratory](https://www.shlab.org.cn/), and earned both my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Computer Science at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT).
