**Visiting Doctoral Student (ELLIS Exchange) **

**Email:** [email protected]

I am a visiting ELLIS PhD candidate at the Institute for Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence and Technology (INSAIT), where I work on robot learning, with a focus on foundation models for robotics, under the supervision of [Prof. Luc Van Gool](https://insait.ai/prof-luc-van-gool/) and [Dr. Danda Paudel](https://insait.ai/dr-danda-paudel/).

I am also a PhD candidate in the Robotics Research Group at [KU Leuven](https://www.kuleuven.be/english/kuleuven), where I have the pleasure of working with [Prof. Renaud Detry](https://www.kuleuven.be/wieiswie/en/person/00144904). My research explores robot perception and control, starting from traditional methods such as SLAM and optimization-based planning. Recently, I have been focusing on data-driven approaches to perception and control.
